Legal Rex: Document Attestation and Apostille Services

Legal Rex offers comprehensive services for document Attestation and Apostille, which are vital processes for authenticating documents for international use.

Service Purpose Requirement
Attestation Verifies the authenticity of documents for international use Required for documents used in most countries (general authentication by local authorities)
Apostille A specialized form of attestation that confirms the document is genuine and can be accepted by other member countries without further authentication Required for documents used in countries that are members of The Hague Convention

The Apostille Process in India

The Apostille process, required for documents to be recognized internationally by Hague Convention countries, can be complex and typically involves multiple steps:

  1. Notary Verification, Attestation and Valid outside India Attestation
  2. State-Level Attestation
  3. Validation from a Designated Authority: In India, this is the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA).
  4. Final Apostille Stamp

Legal Rex: Non-Availability of Birth Certificate (NABC) Assistance

Legal Rex specializes in online assisting individuals with obtaining the Non-Availability of Birth Certificate (NABC), a crucial document for those who cannot secure a traditional birth certificate due to administrative or registration challenges.

Importance of the NABC

The NABC serves as an official acknowledgment of an individual's birth when a standard birth certificate is unavailable. It is essential for various legal and administrative processes:

  • Applying for government identification (IDs).
  • School admissions.
  • Passport applications.
  • Establishing identity, nationality, and eligibility for various benefits and rights.

Reasons for Needing an NABC

Individuals may require an NABC due to reasons such as:

  • Hospital records being lost.
  • Births occurring at home without formal registration.
  • Other administrative challenges preventing the issuance of a traditional certificate.

The Process and Legal Rex's Role

To secure an NABC, individuals must provide evidence of their birth. Legal Rex's experienced legal team guides clients through the collection and submission of the required documentation:
