Document Attestation & Apostille

Comprehensive services for Attestation and Apostille to authenticate documents for international use.

Service Purpose Requirement
Attestation Verifies the authenticity of documents for international use. Required for documents used in most countries (local authentication).
Apostille Specialized form of attestation for Hague Convention countries. Required for member countries of The Hague Convention.

The Apostille Process in India

  1. Notary Verification and initial attestation
  2. State-Level Attestation
  3. Validation by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA)
  4. Final Apostille Stamp

Notary Attestation

Notary verification certifies the authenticity of signatures and documents and is often required before further attestation or apostille steps.
